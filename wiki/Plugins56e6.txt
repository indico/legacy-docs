= Indico Plugins =

== Search ==

=== repozer ===

Repozer provides search capabilities out of the box from within Indico. It uses [http://docs.repoze.org/catalog/ repoze.catalog] as its index. Repozer gives you the ability to search inside Events.

 * **Developer:** Giorgio Pieretti
 * **Repository:** https://github.com/Ictp/Repozer
