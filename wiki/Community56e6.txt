= Community = 
==== Mailing Lists

 * [https://groups.cern.ch/group/project-indico-administrators/default.aspx project-indico-administrators@cern.ch] - Possibly high-volume mailing list, intended for discussion among Indico server administrators (installation problems, bugs, tech questions...). Please, try to follow our guidelines on [wiki:Dev/BugReporting bug reporting]. 
   * You can join the mailing list [http://cern.ch/simba3/SelfSubscription.aspx?userHasAccount=false&groupName=project-indico-administrators here]
   * Unsubscribe from [http://cern.ch/simba3/SelfSubscription.aspx?userHasAccount=false&groupName=project-indico-administrators here]
 * [https://groups.cern.ch/group/project-indico-devel/default.aspx project-indico-devel@cern.ch] - Possibly high-volume mailing list, intended for discussion among Indico developers and hackers.
   * You can join the mailing list [http://cern.ch/simba3/SelfSubscription.aspx?userHasAccount=false&groupName=project-indico-devel here]
   * Unsubscribe from [http://cern.ch/simba3/SelfSubscription.aspx?userHasAccount=false&groupName=project-indico-devel here]

Moderated: no

==== IRC

You can join us at our official **#indico** channel at **Freenode** (`irc.freenode.net`).

If you don't have an IRC client, you can always use Freenode's own [http://webchat.freenode.net/?channels=indico web client].
